Foods of the United States Virgin Islands

~~~~~~~~~~~~

St. Croix ~~ St. Thomas ~~ St. John

I ate at over a 3 dozen different places during the summer, but below are my top 4 favorites

Cafe Fresco ~ 9/10

$$

VIBE: Outdoor patio healthy food

Great salads, get the peanut punch if you want a sweet smoothie pick me up. Food usually is enough for two meals

Ital in Paradise ~ 8.5/10

$$

Cute dining area but good for take-out. Had great healthly juices. In a very walkable place

Lots of food for the money

Chicken Shack ~ 10/10

$$

A pick-up spot, has sitting but better as a grab and go.

Best roasted chicken and Johnny cakes on this island

Armstrong ~ 10/10

$$

No seating, you take it to go.

Best ice cream on the island!!!!

Singhs Fast Food ~ 8/10

$$

A quick walk up and order

Get a Double for $3

Twin Trees ~ 9/10

$$

Small but has a nice outside seating and a view

Get the saltfish pates!

The local ice cream places were the best. There were so many ice creams made with local fruits that I would try to get some every week. Each week I tried a new ice cream flavor.
