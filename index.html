Beaches of the United States Virgin Islands

St. Croix

~~~~~~~~~~~~

During this summer I spent 91 days in the United States Virgin Islands (USVI) as part of a summer internship at the University of the Virgin Islands Research and Technology Park (RTPARK). This is enough days to be considered a full-time resident and get local status. This was interesting to me because a lot of the start-ups and companies that wanted to relocate here needed to get local status but for some it was too hard. The USVI has some of the most diverse history. - the United States brought the USVI in The tourism of the USVI brings in hundreds of thousands each year. The general population of all three islands can double in a night depending on the number of cruise ships that come in.

Estimated Population per Island! St. Croix: est. 42,000 St. Thomas: est. 42,000 St. John: 5,000

Charlotte Amalie in St. Thomas is the captial of the USVI. St. Thomas is extremely hilly and hard to get around unless you have a car.
